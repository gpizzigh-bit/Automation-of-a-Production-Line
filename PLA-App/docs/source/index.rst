Welcome to PLA-App's documentation!
===================================

**Automation of a Production Line (PLA)** is an apllication responsible for Automating the production of a flexible production line simulator, development of a MES (Manufacturing Execution System) to monitor and manage the production on the plant floor and development of an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) module to recieve orders from clients and schedule acquisition orders as well as production orders.
